What's N2TEN?

We are located in Ottawa, Ontario. What makes us different is the way we value people. As a creative digital agency, our goal is to connect people. We value those who are active participants in helping us connect and create deeper relationships in the community. We repay those who help us with our vision with proper reimbursement . We optimize return on investments for every participant in our program - whether you’re a business owner, student, or a partner bringing us a client.

  • Bring Your Own Client Program
  • Student Metro

BYOC - Bring Your Own Client Bring us a client and we'll pay you

It's that simple!

01.

Find a client

We help businesses grow by improving their digital presence (website, logo, video, etc.) Whether you know someone personally or you would like to go out scouting for new clients, take advantage of this opportunity and earn money easily with us!

02.

Become our partner

Once you have a client, message us with the client’s information and we will set up a meeting. As our partner, you will be attending the meeting alongside our most experienced developers and designers.

03.

Be Part of the Creation Process (optional)

If you are skilled in any aspects of design or development, we will give you an opportunity to be part of the creation process. You will gain real-world experiences while working with a skilled team.
